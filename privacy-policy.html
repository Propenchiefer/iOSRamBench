Privacy Policy for RamBench

Last Updated: May 8, 2025

RamBench ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how RamBench, a mobile application ("the App"), handles information. The App benchmarks iOS device RAM usage and displays system entitlement information locally.

1. Information We Collect

RamBench does not collect, store, or transmit personal or identifiable data. It processes the following non-personal data locally:

  • Device Information: RAM usage (e.g., total, used, free) and iOS version for benchmarking.
  • Entitlement Status: System entitlements (e.g., com.apple.developer.kernel.extended-virtual-addressing, com.apple.developer.kernel.increased-memory-limit).
  • Benchmark Results: Local storage of results (e.g., allocated memory in GB, iOS version) via UserDefaults.

This data stays on your device and is not linked to your identity.

2. Use of Information

Non-personal data is used to:

  • Perform and display RAM benchmarks.
  • Show entitlement statuses.
  • Store results locally for your reference.

3. Data Sharing

RamBench does not share or disclose data with third parties, as all processing is local.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. You can clear it via the App’s "Clear Results" feature.

5. Third-Party Services

RamBench does not use third-party analytics, ads, or services that collect data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.
